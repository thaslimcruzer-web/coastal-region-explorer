About

Protecting the Coast We Love

Coastal Region is a smart tourism platform developed to promote sustainable travel in the Pasikudah and Kalkudah coastal regions while preserving their ecological integrity.

We work with local businesses, conservation authorities, and researchers to provide real-time environmental data and support responsible tourism.
